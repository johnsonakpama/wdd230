Join

The Benefit for joining

The local chamber of commerce is a freguently overlooked resource for salespeople, jma chamber of exist to help local business prosper, and for a mordest modest annual fee, we will do everything in power to help sell, it's usually possible to attend chamber events and so on without becoming a member, but the benefits of membership are great that it's well worth the fee to go ahead and sign up.

Networking Opportunities

Chamer event are also terrific networking opportunity. The jma chamber of commerce will usually have regularly scheduled "mixer" in which members can meet and greet each other. you can make the most of these opportunities by volunteering to speak at such a mixer, most chamber member are local small business owner who are likely interested in learning more about sales technigues.
